My name is Donna Delikat and I am the owner and principal photographer of DxD Photography.  I specialize in all aspects of equine photography, including equine and family portraits, competition and action (with or without a rider).  If you have something special in mind, please call us.  It is likely that we can accommodate your request.

Equine photography is more than knowing how to produce an eye-pleasing image and it is more than setting the proper exposure for various light conditions. Equine photography is having a deep understanding of the interaction between horse and owner.

I love photography and I love horses.  My work is more than just being a photographer.  It is about combining two passions into a career that allows me to be creative and provide beautiful images for my clients to love and enjoy for many years.
